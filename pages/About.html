About Me

My name is Molly and I am 30 years old. For as long as I can remember, I was the person who procrastinated through life and wishing for a better way to get things done. When I was pregnant with my daughter, I noticed that there were two types of mothers on the internet that were the loudest:

  • The Crunchy Mom: The mom who can do it all including eating raw oats like a horse and keeping an all-white kitchen spotless
  • The Cool Lazy Mom: I guess there was a backlash to the crunchy mom and created this mom who has an extremely messy house and their kids eat nothing but pixie sticks and soggy chicken nuggets

Where was someone like me? A mom who is a little of both and is just trying their best to survive and thrive? I came to the realization that we were too busy living our lives to give out strange advice and set up a ring light for the thousandth time.

I am a gamer, reader, developer, mother, wife, friend, sister, aunt, niece, and human. I love to cook and stream in my limited free time. Check out some of these other pages to see some things I've picked up over the years. Enjoy!
